About DMND
Smart Technology. Real impact. For people and planet

• Whether you're choosing how to travel, what to eat, or how to use energy, DMND gives you real-time insights, smaerter alternatives, and CO2 rewards you can save, spend, or trade

How does it work?

How does it work?

1. Create Your Personal CO₂ Profile

Set up your account and let DMND learn from your lifestyle — your travel habits, purchases, and energy use

2. Get Your Dynamic CO₂ Budget

DMND calculates a personalized carbon budget that updates in real-time based on your activities, goals, and local conditions

3. Track Your Daily Lifestyle Choices

Every action counts. From commuting and shopping to heating and eating, DMND automatically tracks your carbon footprint

4. Receive Smart, Real-Time Guidance

DMND gives you tailored suggestions in the moment: “Bike today — you’ll save 2kg CO₂ and stay within budget

5. Benefit from Context-Aware Insights

Powered by AI, DMND combines data like weather, traffic, energy prices, and your own patterns to recommend the best sustainable choice at the right time

6. Earn, Save, and Trade CO₂ Credits

Your positive actions generate transparent, blockchain-based carbon credits — which you can keep, trade, or use for rewards

7. See Your Impact & Inspire Change

Track your progress, celebrate your savings, and join a community of changemakers working together to reduce CO₂ and restore balance
