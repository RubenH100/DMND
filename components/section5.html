The DMND Token

Tracking:
Your sustainable actions (like choosing electric transport or reducing energy use) are automatically converted into CO₂ savings.
Verification:
These CO₂ savings are recorded as unique, verifiable transactions on the blockchain.
Rewards:
You earn DMND tokens in return, which you can save, use, or trade within the platform.
Transparency:
Every token is fully traceable and tamper-proof thanks to the decentralized and immutable nature of blockchain technology.
