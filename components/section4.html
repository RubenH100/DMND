DMND Ecosystem

Four core stakeholders form the diamond:
D – Deciders (Consumers): People making daily choices — the engine of behavioural change
M – Makers (Business partners): Providers of sustainable options — mobility, energy, products
N – Nature (The ultimate beneficiary): The reason we build this — to reduce CO₂ and restore balance
D – Drivers (Investors): The visionaries and funders who make innovation, scale, and impact possible
